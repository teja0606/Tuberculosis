About Ravi Teja

Insights into the creator of this AI Assistant.

A Visionary in AI and Education

Ravi Teja is a Senior Generative AI Engineer with over 9 years of experience in teaching and IT. His expertise spans more than 5 years in data science, contributing to innovative solutions leveraging data for intelligent systems.

Currently, Ravi Teja is pursuing a part-time PhD at Lovely Professional University (LPU) under the guidance of Dr. Dalwinder Singh. His doctoral research focuses on advancing generative AI, aiming to provide original insights and methodologies for both industry and academia.

A notable part of his academic contribution includes the "Development of Convolution Neural Networks Model for Detection of Pulmonary Tuberculosis using CT Scan Images." This significant work was submitted by Meka Lakshmi Raviteja, with Registration No: 42300130. This blend of practical engineering leadership and rigorous academic inquiry highlights his comprehensive approach to mastering and evolving the field of artificial intelligence.
